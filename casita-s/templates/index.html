We made our own version of the Birria Tacos from scratch and it wasn't just developed overnight. It took us some time to get the version of Birria Tacos that we serve now. The crispy tortilla, flavorful beef, melted cheese, fresh cilantro, onions, and the hot consomme that we pour over the tacos - every bits of the Birria Tacos was family approved!
